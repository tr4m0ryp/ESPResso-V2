FINAL WAREHOUSE LEG

The user message specifies the target warehouse market: EU or US.

EU warehouses are located in the Benelux/North Germany logistics
corridor: Rotterdam, Hamburg, Antwerp, or Venlo. Choose the most
logical entry point based on the shipping route.

US warehouses are located at major import gateways: Los Angeles area
(for Pacific routes) or New York/New Jersey area (for Atlantic or
Suez routes). Choose based on the origin of the shipment.

If the assembly location is distant from the warehouse, include
intermediate port stops in the leg. For example, assembly in Dhaka
would route through Chittagong Port, then by sea to the warehouse
region, then by road to the final warehouse.

The warehouse is always the final destination. The last leg must end
at the warehouse location.
